Game rules

Warning ! ! ! Don't indulge, you may lose everything.

1.There are three tables in all.

2.You can buy a table without a master for 1 Nas.(1 Nas will be the balance on the table).Or buy a table for someone else to pay 1.5 times the price.(The balance on the table remains unchanged).

3.If someone buys your table, you will get 1.5 times the balance on the table.

4.You can increase or reduce the balance of your own table.(The balance on the table can not be less than 1 Nas.)

5.You can play on someone's desk.

6.According to odds, the maximum amount won is no more than the balance on the table.

7.The money lost by playing games will be increased to the balance on the table, and the owner of the table can be extracted at any time.

8.We will charge 3% Commission.
